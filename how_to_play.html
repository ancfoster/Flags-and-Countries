How to Play

Overview

Countries & Flags is an interactive quiz game designed to allow you to test and develop your knowledge of the flags of the world's countries.

How to complete the game

The game consists of 70 questions and ten levels. To complete the game you must answer all 70 questions correctly.
A game consists of ten levels, each progressively getting harder and featuring more obscure nations. In total there are 220 countries and self-governing territories in the question bank.

The maximum score is 70 points, at which point 210 countries will have been taken from the question bank.

How to play

Flags and Countries is a multiple choice quiz game and the questions are presented in two formats. Simply pick the correct country name or flag to proceed to the next question.

Scores

All of your scores are saved in your web browser, allowing you to track your progress. These will be cleared if you clear your browser cache.
